ICED CAFE LATTE

HISTORY

The term Cafe Latte was created in certain regions of Italy where American tourists frequented. The American travellers struggled with the bitter and richer flavours of espresso so warm milk was added to make a more palatable and sweeter drink. It was in the 1980s that in Seattle, Washington the ‘Latte’ was standardized.

INGREDIENTS

Espresso shot and Fresh Milk

DESCRIPTION

Full-bodied espresso diluted with fresh milk.
